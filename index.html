About LoonieTracker

LoonieTracker was built out of necessity. After years of watching Canadians pay high fees for complex, underperforming mutual funds, we realized the simplest solution was also the best: **All-in-One Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs)**.

These funds, such as VEQT and XEQT, hold thousands of global stocks and bonds in a single ticker, automatically rebalancing for you. They offer maximum diversification with minimum fuss.

Our goal is to provide the clearest, most actionable guides for using these funds within every type of Canadian investment account, paired with a simple spreadsheet to track your progress. Investing should be simple, effective, and cheap.
