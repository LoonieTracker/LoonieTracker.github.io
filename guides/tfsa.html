What is a TFSA?

The Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) is one of the most powerful savings and investment vehicles available to Canadians. Any investment growth (interest, dividends, or capital gains) within a TFSA is completely tax-free, even when you withdraw it. It's the perfect place to house your long-term, high-growth investments.

Wealthsimple allows you to use your TFSA for various investment strategies, including Robo-advising (Managed Investing) or self-directed trading (Wealthsimple Trade).

Step-by-Step Tutorial: Open Your TFSA

Wealthsimple has made the process of opening an account incredibly simple and fast. Follow these steps to set up your Tax-Free Savings Account and start investing:

  1. Start Your Application

    Click on the exclusive referral link here to begin. You will be prompted to enter your email address and create a password.

  2. Choose Your Account Type

    Once logged in, you will be asked what type of account you want to open. Select "TFSA (Tax-Free Savings Account)". You may also be asked whether you want a Managed (Robo) or Self-Directed (Trade) account.

    • **Managed:** Wealthsimple's algorithm chooses investments for you based on your risk profile. (Great for beginners!)
    • **Self-Directed (Trade):** You buy and sell stocks and ETFs yourself.

  3. Provide Personal Information

    You'll need to fill in basic information like your legal name, address, phone number, and Social Insurance Number (SIN). Your SIN is mandatory for tax-advantaged accounts like the TFSA and allows Wealthsimple to report contribution data to the CRA.

  4. Set Up Your Profile and Risk Tolerance (Managed Only)

    If you chose a Managed TFSA, you will answer a short questionnaire to determine your investment goals, time horizon, and risk tolerance (e.g., Conservative, Balanced, Growth). This helps the platform select the perfect portfolio of ETFs for you.

  5. Fund Your Account

    Connect your bank account instantly via Plaid or manually using account numbers. Initiate your first transfer. Remember: only contribute funds if you are certain you have contribution room available, which can be checked on your CRA My Account.

  6. Verify Identity (If Required)

    In some cases, Wealthsimple may require you to upload a photo of your government ID or verify your identity through a brief online process. This is for regulatory compliance.

  7. Start Investing!

    Once your funds arrive (which may take a few business days), your money will be invested automatically (Managed) or you can begin placing trades (Trade). Congratulations, you now have a tax-free investment account!

Common Questions

Is Wealthsimple TFSA safe?

Yes. Wealthsimple is a registered investment firm. Your accounts are held in trust and protected by the Canadian Investor Protection Fund (CIPF) within specified limits.

What is my TFSA contribution limit?

Your total contribution room depends on your age (must be 18 or older), residency, and previous contributions. For the most accurate number, always check your **CRA My Account**. Over-contributing can result in penalties.

What can I invest in within a Wealthsimple TFSA?

With Wealthsimple Trade, you can invest in thousands of stocks and ETFs listed on Canadian and US exchanges (with no commission fees). With Wealthsimple Managed, you invest in a pre-built, diversified portfolio of ETFs.
